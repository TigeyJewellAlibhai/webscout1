DARC SIDE SCOUTING 2019

Welcome to the DARC SIDE webscout system! This system is designed to allow a team to collect and utilize valuabe data on games and teams in FIRST Robotics Competitions. By implementing a multi-stage process, data can be collected from phones and analyzed instantly on a computer with an highly automized process. Quick pre-game strategies can be formulated easily, while long term analytics can also be developed. The system can also use stats to develop tailored pick lists for alliance captains.

To view data and statistics you need permission to access the google sheets file where the data is processed. If you are interested in using this system for scouting, please contact us by email at
