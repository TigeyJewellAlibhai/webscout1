MATCH SCOUTING

In match scouting, a scout will record data on one team per match. This system uses a google form, one response recorded per robot scouted. Choose an option below to begin scouting a team. The web based form and google form are identical, but the google form may not work with your device or google account. Please make all data as accurate as possible. All data is property of team 6502.

PIT SCOUTING

In match scouting, a team of scouts will record data by observing a robot in the pit. When pit scouting, it is important to be polite to the team being scouted. Usually having one person asking the questions and another recording data is best. This system uses a google form, one response recorded per robot scouted. Choose an option below to begin scouting a team. The web based form and google form are identical, but the google form may not work with your device or google account. Please make all data as accurate as possible. All data is property of team 6502.
